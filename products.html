Build Your Own Practice (BYOP)

Opening a private practice can be scary. Many mental health professionals spend their internships (and maybe part of their paid career) at community mental health agencies. Most graduate schools focus on creating the best mental health clinicians (i.e. they discuss human development, therapy modalities/theories, etc.). But, nowhere along the way are you taught about the business side of therapy. I’m here to change that. I graduated in December of 2020, launched my practice in March of 2021, and now have a thriving, 100% virtual private practice. I’m here to show you that it is possible to make the leap from grad school (or CMH) to private practice. You can do it, you can be successful, and earn what you're worth. So, if you’re a mental health practitioner who’s ready to make the leap, then this course is for you.
